2003 Chevy SSR

The 2003 was the first year of production for the SSR which included the 5.3 LS engine, 4L60E automatic transmission. The interior was only available in Ebony w/Ultrasoft Nuance Leather Seating Surfaces [Black].

Color options available:
  • Redline Red
  • Slingshot Yellow
  • Smokin Asphalt
2004 Chevy SSR

The 2004 started off with carryover 2003 modes from late in the calendar year, no changes were made to performance, interior, or exterior design.

Color options available:
  • Redline Red
  • Ricochet Silver Metallic
  • Slingshot Yellow
  • Smokin Asphalt
  • Ultra Violet Metallic
2005 Chevy SSR

The 2005 upgraded the powertrain from the 5.3 LS to the 6.0 LS 2 engine from the Corvette again designs and features remained largely unchanged.

Color options available:
  • Aqua Blur Metallic
  • Redline Red
  • Ricochet Silver Metallic
  • Slingshot Yellow
  • Smokin Asphalt
2006 Chevy SSR

The 2006 was the final production year for the SSR and along with additional interior colors options that matched some of the body colors, the 6.0 LS engine a 6-speed manual transmission was also offered during this final year.

Color options available:
  • Aqua Blur Metallic
  • Pacific Blue Metallic
  • Redline Red
  • Ricochet Silver Metallic
  • Slingshot Yellow
  • Smokin Asphalt
